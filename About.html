    About Us

    ABOUT DEVNAIJA

    Devnaija is a programming company that aims to radically transform the
    output of education in africa through tech education. We have reached
    over 6000 youths and children in Nigeria preparing them for the future
    of work with the core skills in programming and robotics. Our hands on,
    project-based and peer learning approach is helping to build brilliant
    that will unleash globally competitive innovations from Africa.

    VISION

    Devnaija has the vision to see one million people benefitting from the
    from the school worldwide by 2050. The organization aims to achieve this goal by
    providing education and training while being affordable to all.

    MISSION

    DEVNAIJA will create a world clss school with a difference by focusing
    on coding amd financial literacy. Devnaija strives to provide the highest
    quality of education and training, in an enviroment that promotes creativity,
    innovation, leadershiip and financial literacy.

    OUR VALUES

    Integrity:We want to be known for the truth and being acknowledged
    for being trustworthy by the people and the nation. We place a high value on the
    character and integrity of the people that work with us and our business
    engagements.

    Innovation:We beliee that tomorrow should always be better than
    today, so we work hard to ensure that we constantly improve learning
    and bring newness to all our endeavours.

    Intelligence:We appreciate brilliance at all levels and ensure that we
    always go for the best in people.
